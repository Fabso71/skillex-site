How SkillEX works

The concept is simple: show that gaming develops valuable skills and make those skills visible to employers.

1. Connect your profile

Users add their gaming background, favorite titles, competitive history, and playstyle.

2. Translate skills

SkillEX identifies transferable strengths such as teamwork, communication, problem-solving, discipline, and adaptability.

3. Create opportunities

Companies can explore player profiles and discover talent with proven performance habits.

For Gamers

Gaming is more than entertainment. It can build real skills that deserve recognition.

Why gamers would use SkillEX

  • Create a profile based on gaming experience
  • Show leadership, teamwork, strategy, and communication in a new way
  • Get noticed by companies that value performance and mindset
  • Stand out without relying only on a traditional CV

For Companies

SkillEX helps companies discover motivated candidates through demonstrated performance patterns from gaming.

Benefits for employers

  • Access a new pool of talent
  • Identify soft skills through gaming behavior
  • Find candidates with discipline, focus, and adaptability
  • Build stronger, more diverse teams

Target industries

  • Esports and gaming organizations
  • Technology and software companies
  • Digital marketing agencies
  • Startups looking for competitive, coachable talent
